# MermaidSketch Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

## Overview
MermaidSketch is a visual diagramming app. 
We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not store your diagrams on any server
- We do not track your usage

## AI Feature (Optional)
When you use AI diagram generation:
- Your text prompt is sent to Google Gemini API
- This uses YOUR OWN Gemini API key
- We do not store or access your prompts
- Google's privacy policy applies to API calls

## Local Storage
All diagrams are stored locally on your device 
using iOS secure storage. We have no access 
to your data.

## Third Party Services
- Google Gemini API (only when you use AI feature)
  Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
